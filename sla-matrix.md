# Service Level Agreement (SLA)

| Issue Type | Priority | Response Time | Resolution Time |
|------------|---------|----------------|-----------------|
| System Down | Critical | 30 minutes | 4 hours |
| Login Issue | High | 1 hour | 8 hours |
| Software Error | Medium | 4 hours | 24 hours |
| General Request | Low | 24 hours | 3 days |
